Soos Tuber Market
Open: All day bright days. CLOSED on dark days.
The Soos Tuber Market opened last year, offering a wide selection of tubers grown in the marshlands on the wet side of Soos. Meet all sorts of people here, from Soosians to Earthlings!

Carn Farmer's Market
Open: 1st and 10th of every Thobos cycle.
The Carn Farmer's Market has been a staple of the local food movement since 2446. Located in Maubu's capitol, the market is known for its diverse selection of soil-grown produce.

Lower Tenses Crop Exchange
Open: Most days.
The Lower Tenses Crop Exchange is a subterranean bazaar. Produce here tends to be nutrient-rich, high quality fare, albeit on the pricey side. Skilled shoppers may also participate in the Crop Exchange, where rare produce may be bartered and traded.

Faa Organic Market
Open: 5th of every other Thobos Cycle.
This market is located on the outer rings of Faa and features mostly soil-grown vegetables and some fruit. The market only opened recently, but it is growing rapidly.
