Research Team Identifies Missing Sequence Of Lost 'Potato' Genome.

A research group in the Core has reportedly recovered the last 10% of the gene sequence for the 'potato' crop. The potato was discovered last year by archaeologists in the Ki star system. The potato is believed to be an ancient ancestor of the muu, capable of growing directly from soil. Moreover biologists believe it may not have originated in Ki. Instead, they hypothesize that it may be traced back to the Proxima Centauri system, or a nearby star.
