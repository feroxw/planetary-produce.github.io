Growing your own food can be daunting; it's time-consuming, and it's less predictable than artifically grown food. Still, the rewards are immeasurable, from health benefits, to our safety, and to the increased sustainability of our star system.

There are 3 keys to growing your own food:
  • Balanced soil composition
  • Moisture
  • Maintaining a moderate temperature
